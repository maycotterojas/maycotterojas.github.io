Hello, my name is (Julia) Maycotte-Rojas. I am a first-year Systems Design Engineering student at the University of Waterloo. Considering the human condition is key when creating a system whose goal is to serve society. My goal is to attain the embodiment of the experience when systems are being made, through every step of the design process. Valuing human interaction between systems - comes from my incessant observing that makes me hungry to know more about how these structural forms can interact with fluidity; by considering how human form interacts with these systems. Evidenced by understanding people through form, and thoughtful improvements of existing systems and interactions - via engineering.
